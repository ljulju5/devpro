Small Business Programs 2 Edition (Edicija “Mali Poslovni Programi 2” – MPP2)

The Small Business Programs 2 edition is a collection of software modules dedicated to various aspects of business operations which can be integrated into a single business system. It is a direct continuation of the Small Business Programs 1 Edition which has been the best-seller on the market for several years. Areas of business covered by MPP are:

  • Financial accounting
  • Inventory and Material accounting
  • VAT Calculation
  • Bank Statements and cash flow
  • Fixed Assets
  • Payrolls and Compensation Calculations
  • Production Management
  • Interest Calculations
  • Vehicle Management
  • Warehouse Management
  • Additional modules
